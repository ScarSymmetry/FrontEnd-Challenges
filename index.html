About Us

At our restaurant we believe that wine should be enjoyed, which is why each wine that we carry is available by the glass. Our wine list changes regularly and always includes an array of both old and new world wines. We also serve a selection of classic cocktails and craft beer. In case you're hungry we offer a variety of artisanal cheeses and charcuterie. Our small plates, pizzas, and salads are created with an emphasis on flavor and prepared with quality, fresh ingredients.
